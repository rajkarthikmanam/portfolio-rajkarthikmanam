About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida and a Teaching Assistant for Python for Business. I design and build ML systems end-to-end—curating data, training and evaluating models (CNNs, transformer-based NLP, classical ML), and packaging results into simple APIs or clear visuals that people can use.

I write production-quality code in Java, Python, and C, with working knowledge of R and C#. My work spans computer vision, NLP, and predictive analytics. I care as much about clean, reproducible experiments as I do about model accuracy—and I’m happiest taking a problem from idea → prototype → shipped solution.

I bring strong math and problem-solving, clear communication from my TA role, and a builder’s mindset. I’m looking for roles where I can turn data and modern ML into products that make decisions faster and better.
