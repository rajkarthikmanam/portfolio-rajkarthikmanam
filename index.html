About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida and a Teaching Assistant for Python for Business. I design and build ML solutions end-to-end—preparing data, training and evaluating models (CNNs, transformer-based NLP, classical ML), and delivering results through simple APIs or clear dashboards.

I write production-quality code in Java, Python, and C, with working knowledge of R and C#. I’m comfortable with SQL (data querying, joins, analytics pipelines) and visualization in Tableau and Power BI. I care about clean, reproducible experiments and shipping solutions that people actually use.

I bring strong mathematical aptitude and logical problem-solving, plus a solid grounding in cryptography fundamentals that informs my view on data security. I’m looking for roles where I can turn data and modern ML into products that make decisions faster and better.
