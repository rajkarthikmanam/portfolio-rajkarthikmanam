About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida and a Teaching Assistant for Python for Business. I ship end-to-end ML solutions—preparing data, training and evaluating models (CNNs, transformer-style NLP, classical ML), and exposing results via lean APIs or clear dashboards.
